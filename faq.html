Quick Answers

Common Student Questions

Who should I contact if I cannot open the website?

Contact the Lab Support Team first. Share your browser, VPN status, and the URL you are trying to open.

Who can help with CTF challenge questions?

Use the Faculty Coordinator for question access or scoring policy. Use Lab Support for technical issues with submissions.

Where do I find documentation and setup notes?

Open the Wiki page from the navigation bar. It contains architecture, logs, simulations, device management, and operational notes.

Can I report a broken demo or video?

Yes. Contact Lab Support and mention whether the Agriculture demo or Water Distribution demo is failing.
