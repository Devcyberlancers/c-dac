NameSmart AgricultureSmart Water Management

Application TypeSmart agriculture monitoring and automation dashboard for irrigation, soil moisture, water level, pH correction, rain simulation, drainage, and greenhouse/field operations.Water distribution monitoring, CTF, and operations wiki

Network ProtocolsHTTP/REST API, MQTT, WebSocket, Wi-Fi 2.4 GHz, Modbus TCP. Router used: TP-Link TL-WR845N, 2.4 GHz, 300 Mbps, Wi-Fi 4. [HTTP, MQTT, Modbus TCP, RS485]
Primary SensorsSoil moisture, water level, pH, temperature, humidity, flame/fire alert, LDR/photoresistor, IR obstacle, RFID/NFC, N/P/K dashboard values.
